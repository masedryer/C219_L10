Vaccination Progress

For every 100 people in Singapore, 88 have received their first dose, and 87 are fully vaccinated as of 14 Dec.

In addition, the Health Ministry reported that as of 14 Dec, 96 percent of the eligible population (aged 12 years and above) have completed their full regime, or received two doses of Covid-19 vaccines. With new Covid-19 variants such as Omicron surfacing, full vaccination will have to include booster shots as well.
